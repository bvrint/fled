Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about FLED? Here are some common questions from teachers and parents about our Family-Link for Education and Development platform.

1. How do parents receive updates about their child's attendance?

Parents receive instant push notifications on their mobile devices when teachers mark attendance. They can also view attendance history in real-time through the mobile app, with options to filter by month and export records.

2. Can parents communicate directly with teachers through the app?

Yes! Our two-way messaging system allows parents to reply to teacher messages and announcements. Teachers can see all replies in threaded conversations and respond back, creating meaningful dialogue about student progress and concerns.

3. How does the system handle task scores and grades?

Teachers can create various task types (assignments, quizzes, exams, projects, homework) with color-coded categories. Scores are automatically tracked and parents can view their child's grades in real-time, including overall averages and detailed breakdowns by task type.

4. Is the system secure and private for student data?

Absolutely. FLED uses Firebase security rules to ensure parents only access their own children's data. Teachers authenticate via Google OAuth, and all data is stored securely in the cloud with strict access controls and encryption.

5. Can I manage multiple students from one parent account?

Yes! Parents with multiple children can link all their students to a single account. The system automatically loads all linked students, allowing parents to easily switch between children and view each student's attendance, tasks, and messages in one convenient location.
